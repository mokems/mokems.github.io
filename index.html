Hey! I am Luca Jiang-Tao Yu, a PhD student at AIoT Lab, CDS, HKU in Hong Kong, advised by Prof. Chenshu Wu. Previously, I was a research assistant at MARS Lab, IIIS, THU and Shanghai Qi Zhi Institute in Shanghai and Beijing, China, advised by Prof. Hang Zhao. Before that, I was a research intern at Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications in Beijing, China, advised by Prof. Anfu Zhou.

My research focuses on:

  • wireless sensing, encompassing radar, Wi-Fi, and acoustic sensing applications.
  • signal processing for sensing, including deep learning and signal processing co-design.
  • human-centric AI, particularly in edge device-based healthcare and human-computer interaction.
